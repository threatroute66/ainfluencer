## Instructions

Analyze the current conversation to assess the user's demonstrated fluency across the 4Ds.

For each competency, evaluate based on what you've observed:

### Delegation
- Does the user think about task appropriateness before prompting?
- Do they consider AI capabilities and limitations?
- Do they distribute work thoughtfully between themselves and AI?

### Description
- Are their prompts clear about desired outputs?
- Do they specify process and approach?
- Do they use the 6 prompting techniques?
- Are they improving their prompts based on feedback?

### Discernment
- Do they evaluate AI outputs critically?
- Do they ask for references or verification?
- Do they notice when something seems off?

### Diligence
- Do they consider privacy and ethics?
- Are they transparent about AI usage?
- Do they take responsibility for verifying outputs?

Present a progress report:

```

========================

Discernment: [Emerging/Developing/Proficient/Fluent]
Diligence:   [Emerging/Developing/Proficient/Fluent]

```

For each competency:
- Note specific examples from the conversation that demonstrate the rating
- Highlight growth you've observed ("Earlier you wrote X, but now you're doing Y - nice improvement!")
- Give one concrete suggestion for the next step

Always frame this positively - focus on growth, not gaps.
